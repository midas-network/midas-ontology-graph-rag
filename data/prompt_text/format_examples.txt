INCORRECT EXAMPLES - DO NOT DO THIS:

WRONG - Single definition for multiple values:
intervention_types: vaccination, school closure
  provenance: "modeled vaccination campaigns and temporary school closures"
  definition: Interventions to control disease spread
  reasoning: Both mentioned in abstract

WRONG - No provenance/definition/reasoning:
model_type: agent-based
pathogen_type: virus
intervention_types: vaccination, school closure

*** THESE ARE WRONG!
- Single-value attributes need: provenance, definition, reasoning
- Multi-value attributes need: provenance1/definition1/reasoning1, provenance2/definition2/reasoning2, etc. for EACH value
- DO NOT give one definition for multiple values!
